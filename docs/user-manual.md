# TransitLK User Manual

## Login

1. Open `http://localhost:5173`.
2. Enter your work email and password (`password123` for demo accounts).
3. Click **Sign in to TransitLK**.

## Dashboard (Administrator, Depot manager)

1. Click **Dashboard** in the sidebar.
2. Review route, fleet, and driver summary cards.
3. Check maintenance alerts and recent trips.

## Routes (Administrator, Transport scheduler)

### View routes

1. Click **Routes**.
2. Search or filter by status and service type.
3. Use **Previous** / **Next** to change pages.

### Add route

1. Click **Add route**.
2. Enter route number, start point, end point, and distance.
3. Select service type and set status to **Draft** or **Active**.
4. Add stops if needed.
5. Click **Save route**.

### Assign bus and driver

1. Open a route.
2. Click **Assign bus & driver**.
3. Select a bus and a driver (both required if assigning).
4. Click **Save assignment**.

## Fleet and drivers (Administrator, Fleet manager)

### Add bus

1. Click **Fleet & Drivers**.
2. Open the **Fleet Inventory** tab.
3. Click **Add New Vehicle**.
4. Fill registration number, capacity, mileage, service type, and status.
5. Save the form.

### Add driver

1. Open the **Driver Personnel** tab.
2. Click **Add New Driver**.
3. Fill name, licence, contact, working hours, and status.
4. Enter email and password for My trips login.
5. Save the form.

## Users (Administrator)

1. Click **Users**.
2. Click **Add staff user**.
3. Enter name, email, password, role, and depot.
4. Save.

## Admins (Superadministrator)

1. Click **Admins**.
2. Click **Add administrator**.
3. Enter name, email, password, and depot.
4. Save.

## Depots (Superadministrator)

1. Click **Depots**.
2. Click **Add depot**.
3. Enter depot code, name, region, and location.
4. Save.

## Schedules

### View timetable (Scheduler, Administrator, Depot manager)

1. Click **Schedules**.
2. Switch between **Day**, **Week**, and **Month**.
3. Move dates with the arrow controls.

### Create timetable (Scheduler, Administrator)

1. Click **Create timetable**.
2. Select date range and routes.
3. Set departure and arrival times for each trip.
4. Assign bus and driver to each row.
5. Fix any conflict errors.
6. Save.

### Submit for approval (Scheduler, Administrator)

1. Open a draft trip.
2. Click **Submit for approval**.

### Approve or reject (Depot manager, Administrator)

1. Click **Pending approvals** on Schedules.
2. Click **Approve** on a trip, or **Reject** and enter a reason.

### Driver issues (Scheduler, Administrator)

1. Open **Driver issues** on Schedules.
2. Select a reported trip.
3. Click **Adjust trip**.
4. Update bus, driver, times, or status and save.

## My trips (Driver)

1. Sign in with driver email and password.
2. Click **Start** when the trip begins.
3. Click **Report issue** and enter details if there is a problem.
4. Click **Completed** when the trip ends.

## Maintenance (Administrator, Fleet manager)

1. Click **Maintenance**.
2. Open **Maintenance logs** or **Fuel logs**.
3. Click **Add**.
4. Select bus, enter date, description, and cost.
5. Save.

## Analytics (Superadministrator, Administrator, Scheduler, Depot manager)

1. Click **Analytics**.
2. Select weekly, monthly, or custom dates.
3. Click **PDF** or **CSV** to download.

## Notifications

1. Click the bell icon in the top bar.
2. Open an alert to go to the related screen.

## Sign out

1. Click the profile icon.
2. Select **Sign out**.
